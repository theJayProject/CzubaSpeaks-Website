Welcome To CzubaSpeaks!

CzubaSpeaks is designed to help you learn the English language in a way that differs for everyone.

We believe there is no static way to learn the English language therefore we try our best to find the best way for you!

Our methods of teaching include traditional textbook teaching, newly formed learning through games, conversations and many more.

We want your experience with learning English to be unique and worth remembering.
